BT Sales Analysis

This project provides a detailed visualization of key trends and insights derived from BT's car sales data. It leverages a comprehensive dataset of over 200 recorded and documented customer interactions, which was analyzed to explore various factors influencing sales dynamics. The charts below highlight critical findings related to customer behaviors, demographics, and sales patterns, aiming to pinpoint the key drivers of purchases. The visualizations encompass a range of metrics, including credit applications, sales distribution by day, customer ethnicity, reasons for market entry, MaxCare purchase trends, the impact of pre-qualification, gender distribution, and the influence of car transfers
